ALLAMAGOOSA

Premise: You're a crewmember on a spaceship, and because the spaceship is headed back to Earth, all significant items will have to be accounted for. The head inspector has ticked off all save one -- an 'off-og'. He has no idea what it is and has tasked you to find this missing item before the spaceship makes a timeleap back to Earth. That leaves you with precisely 10 hours.

You can begin by going around the ship to look for the item and ask people questions. Enter "help" to see the commands.


A short story by Eric Frank Russell
