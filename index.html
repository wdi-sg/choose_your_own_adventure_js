Instructions:

Get to the top of the dungeon before losing all your health!
The dungeon has 5 levels! And 7 paths!
The path you chose decides whether you will walk a BATTLE PATH or a PEACE PATH!

BATTLE PATHS are filled with monsters you have to defeat! You have ULTIMATES that will help you get through this ordeal!
PEACE PATHS are filled with traps! So take the correct path to avoid the traps!
