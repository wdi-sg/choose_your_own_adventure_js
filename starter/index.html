Harry Potter Duelling Game

Intro

Welcome to the wonderful wizarding world of Harry Potter! You can choose to be either on the light or dark side of this incredible and duel using your favourite heroes or villains in an all-out 3V3 duel! During each game, after the heroes and villains have been chosen, they will battle it out by taking turns to attack. The impact of the attack will decrease the Health Points(HP) of the character they are attacking. The impact is based on the attack power and the weakness of the character attacked to the attribute of the attack as well as the luckStat of the attacker.

Tips: Do take note that each character has different attributes and attacks. Tailor your attacks to the attributes of your enemy!

Attributes and their rankings

There are 7 attributes in total:

  1. Charms
  2. DADA (Defence Against the Dark Arts)
  3. DA (Dark Arts)
  4. Flying
  5. Herbology
  6. Potions
  7. Tranfiguration

They are weak to each other in the manner shown here: Charms > DADA > DA > Flying > Herbology > Potions > Transfiguration > Charms
